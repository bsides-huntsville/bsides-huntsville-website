About

BSides Huntsville is a community-driven information security conference built by and for the local and regional security, hacker, and maker communities.

BSides is about participation, not passive attendance. Participants are encouraged to share knowledge, ask questions, build things, break things, and learn from one another in an inclusive and welcoming environment.

BSides Huntsville is an independent event operating under the global BSides framework. Sponsors support the community but do not influence content or speaker selection.

Call for Presenters (CFP)

We run a fair and open CFP. Talks, workshops, hands-on labs, demos, and villages are all welcome.

No speaking slots are sold. Sponsors and non-sponsors submit through the same process and are evaluated equally based on educational value.

Code of Conduct

BSides Huntsville is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment for all participants. Harassment or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.

Full Code of Conduct and reporting contacts will be published before the event.

BSides Huntsville operates in coordination with the BSides steering committee and follows official BSides organizer guidelines.

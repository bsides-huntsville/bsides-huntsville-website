About

Security BSides is a community-driven framework for building events by and for members of the information security community. BSides Huntsville brings that spirit to the Tennessee Valley with practical content, friendly people, and a strong focus on learning.

BSides Huntsville is an independent, community-organized event operating under the global BSides framework. While sponsors support the event financially, they do not influence content, speaker selection, or conference operations.

Content

Sessions ranging from beginner-friendly to deeply technical.

Conversation

Opportunities to meet, talk, and learn together during and between sessions.

Community

Local builders, defenders, researchers, and students—everyone’s welcome.

Call for Presenters (CFP)

BSides Huntsville operates a fair, open, and community-driven Call for Presenters. All proposals are reviewed based on content quality, relevance, and educational value.

No speaking slots are sold. Sponsors and non-sponsors submit proposals through the same CFP process and are evaluated equally.

We welcome submissions from first-time speakers, experienced researchers, practitioners, and community members across offensive, defensive, hardware, RF, cloud, AppSec, OSINT, and related domains.

Sponsors

Sponsors make community conferences possible by supporting accessible registration costs and shared spaces. BSides Huntsville is not a lead-generation event.

Participant information is never sold or shared. Badge scanning, contact harvesting, or similar tracking mechanisms are not permitted.

Sponsorship represents a commitment to the local security community and enables networking, conversation, and learning during the event.

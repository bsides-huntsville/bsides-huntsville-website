About

BSides Huntsville is a local, independent, community-driven cybersecurity conference. Everyone is encouraged to participate — whether through talks, workshops, villages, or hallway conversations.

We welcome first-time speakers, students, professionals, hobbyists, and anyone curious about security, hacking, making, and learning.

Call for Presenters (CFP)

We run a fair and open CFP. Talks, workshops, villages, and demos are all welcome. Sponsors do not receive speaking slots.
